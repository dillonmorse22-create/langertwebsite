Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

The Langert Commercial Group, Inc. ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through this website, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

  • Information you provide — when you submit our consultation form or contact us by phone or email, we collect the details you share, such as your name, email address, phone number, and the nature of your inquiry.
  • Automatically collected information — like most websites, our hosting provider and embedded services may log standard technical data such as IP address, browser type, pages visited, and referring URLs.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide commercial real estate brokerage and advisory services.
  • To communicate with you about properties, listings, or services you have expressed interest in.
  • To operate, maintain, and improve this website.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

Third-party services

This website embeds services from third parties that may collect data subject to their own privacy policies, including YouTube (video playback), Google Fonts, Elfsight (Google reviews display), and Unsplash (imagery). Video embeds and review widgets may set cookies or receive technical data when they load.

Data retention

We retain inquiry information only as long as needed to respond to you and conduct our business, or as required by law and real estate recordkeeping regulations.

Your choices

You may contact us at any time to ask what information we hold about you, request a correction, or request deletion, by emailing elangert@thelangertgroup.com or calling (443) 529-0505.

Children's privacy

This website is intended for business audiences and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date above reflects the most recent revision.
